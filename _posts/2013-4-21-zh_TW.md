## Commands /rpgitem 

### /rpgitem <span style='color:#b5e853'>help</span> <span style='color:#006EFF'>分類</span><span style='color:#1BE0BF'>[字符]</span>

### /rpgitem <span style='color:#1BE0BF'>[物品]</span>
